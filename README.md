### Realistic Echocardiogram Generation using GANs  
**Conference:** IEEE SIPAIM 2025  

This research presents a comparative evaluation of generative and reconstruction deep learning architectures for synthetic cardiac ultrasound image generation, aiming to support medical simulation, training, and diagnostic research.
- Comparative study of generative models under controlled training conditions  
- Focus on small dataset learning and medical realism  
- Quantitative and qualitative evaluation
